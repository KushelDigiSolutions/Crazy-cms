what we do

ROI Advertising Agency wants to know what is important to you so we can be sure your message is received in the way you want. We are a young but extremely talented agency that listens then responds.

The biggest thing that differentiates us from other agencies is that we believe in the human experience. AI cannot understand how your product or service really feels, tastes, how impactful it can be amongst so many other things. So we use human ingenuity to create and bring your vision to life, knowing that AI will never know your product or service like a human can.

As our name (ROI Advertising Agency) implies, our goal is to give you the impact you are looking from your advertising dollars. Once you see the quality we can generate, you will see that your advertising dollars were wisely spent.

Whether you are looking for:

  • Branding
  • Increasing Sales
  • Regaining Market Share
  • Reminding people about your product or service(s)
  • Introducing a new product
  • Existing product wanting a presence in the United States
  • To draw attention and prompt immediate action
  • Maintaining your contact with your customers and stakeholders

Professional Team Founding partners

STEVE DOLL

CEO, ROI Advertising Agency

Steve Doll CEO– Has always had a desire to be in advertising, the desire to help a company to reach its goals and surpass them. Prior to starting his career in Marketing, Steve Doll was a veteran of the US NAVY. Having a history of working for companies like Gannett, Auto Trader, Verizon, and Hearst Media, he had a "I can do this and more importantly I can do this better" mentality started ROI Advertising Agency. He possessed the drive and desire to do things better for his clients and his concepts were what launched ROI Advertising Agency, knowing he can put together a team that is capable and knowledgeable to get your message to the customers.

Jim Hulse

COO, ROI Advertising Agency

Jim Hulse COO – Jim likes nothing more than showing off his creativity to the world. He realizes that being a part of ROI allows him to do just that. Not only is Jim a US Army Veteran, but he also graduated from Daemen University with Magna Cum Laude honors. He has a BFA in animation and has even served the university as an adjunct instructor. Perhaps most notably, Jim's senior film won a Gold Telly Award in the Student/Non-Broadcast category. Jim was the first 2D animator from his university to accomplish the feat. ROI Advertising is fortunate to have his artistic and creative skills to serve all your branding and advertising needs.
