Raise Funds for Your Organization with F.I.R.S.T.

F.I.R.S.T. offers a unique fundraising opportunity for nonprofits and community groups.

We partner with 14 popular local restaurants to offer $25 dining gift certificates. Your organization can sell these certificates for $25 and keep $8 for each certificate sold.

How It Works

  • Your group sells these certificates at face value and keeps $8 per certificate for your organization.
  • F.I.R.S.T. provides you the certificates with no upfront money required.
  • Your organization keeps the $8 difference as profit!

Benefits:

  • Requires no upfront costs - only pay for certificates once sold
  • Certificates never expire, so they make great gifts
  • Supports local restaurants while raising money
  • Easy for supporters - one certificate works at 14 locations

Additional Fundraising Options

  • Sell certificates at events, meetings, and more
  • Offer as a thank you gift for donors
  • Bundle into gift baskets for auctions
  • Give as prizes and rewards

F.I.R.S.T. provides a turnkey solution to earn substantial revenue for your organization. We are here to help you with your sales and promotions.
