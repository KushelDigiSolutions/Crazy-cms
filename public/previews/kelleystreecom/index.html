Welcome!  Kelley's Tree Service


Welcome to Kelley's Tree Service

Having trees in your yard just gives more life to your home. They provide natural shade, bring you more air to breathe, and they just keep your home fresh at all times. They are also witnesses to your family’s growth over the years. Like many other plants in your garden, trees need love and care so that they are able to live longer and stronger. It is therefore an imperative that you keep them at their best shape at by means of trimming and pruning. But these chores are can take a lot of time and effort, and it is understandable if you are not able to maintain them regularly.

But what if we can provide the right tree service that you need? If you’re currently looking for a reliable company that would provide you with quality tree service solutions, then you’re at the right place.

Owned and operated by Kelley Marranca, our tree service solutions have been in the business for more than a decade already. We have a certified arborists who knows how to keep your trees in the best conditions; they are not just simply trimmed-their health is preserved inside and out.

We provide different kinds of tree service packages, including:

  • Tree Removal
  • Tree Planting
  • Land Clearing
  • Pruning
  • Stump Grinding
  • Firewood
  • Campfire Wood
  • Gutter cleaning

Why should you choose us as your tree service provider? Aside from providing quality services since 2000, our team is also expert on the different methods to prune, remove and stump your trees and bushes. We also understand situations in which you need to have your tree branches clipped or your trees entirely removed, and because of these we are open for business 24 hours a day, 7 days a week….and yes…all year around! You don’t have to wait for days just to have your fallen trees and branches cleared; we can render the right tree service according to your needs and preferences at your most convenient time.
