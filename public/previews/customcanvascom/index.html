Welcome To Custom Canvas

Renowned for our custom canvas products for your home or business, Custom Canvas of Buffalo New York is also able to create just about any canvas covers that you need. Creating custom canvas for any size and any purpose, we use the finest canvas on the market today. We'll never cut corners or try to use the cheaper material for your projects.

Though we are based as an truck cover manufacturer in Buffalo, New York, we service the entire area, including but not limited to Western New York, Buffalo, Clarence, Tonawanda and we create larger custom canvas in Erie Pennsylvania.

Custom Canvas Manufacturing Co., Inc. offers the very best materials and craftsmanship in our complete line of canvas tent products. Whether you need a backyard party tent or a 60 by 30-foot industrial tent, you have our guarantee that the same quality and durability goes into your customized canvas tent.

Our experienced team of professionals at Custom Canvas has merged the values of yesterday with today's state-of-the-art techniques and equipment, giving you the best product we can offer - with pride
