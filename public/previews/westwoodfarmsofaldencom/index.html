Vegetables we are growing this season:

Asparagus, Lettuce x 8 different varieties, Spinach, Broccoli, Carrots, Cauliflower, Brussels Sprouts, Beans, Cabbage, (4) Heirloom varieties of Garlic (planted last fall), Leeks, Heirloom Tomatoes, Hybrid Tomatoes, Cherry Tomatoes, Hot peppers, Bell Peppers, Beets, Burpless Cucumber, Zucchini, Pumpkins, Melons, Potatoes, Onions, Super Sweet Corn, Watermelon, Bok Choy, Turnips, Peas, Kale... Rosemary, Basil, Chives, Parsley's, Thyme, Dill etc.

Farmstand; Daily Harvests from the garden available at our roadside stand. Vegetables, Fruits, Cut Flowers, perennials, birdhouses, shrubs, vegetable plants & cherry Firewood

CSA (Community Supported Agriculture); Allows community members to know their farmer and their food. Shareholders pick up their fresh produce on a weekly basis and are then able to see exactly how their food is being produces.

WHAT WE’RE DOING

Products We Offer

Smoker Rental

You'll also be able to order: Free Range whole Chickens, Beef, Turkey & Pork.

Asparagus

This perennial favorite is so tender; you'll never look at store bought the same.

Lettuce and spring Greens

Our first harvest of the season from our seed bed. A blend of Lettuces from Romaine's to Buttercrunch. Spinach tops off this early spring delicacy.

Strawberries

Starting in early June we offer the sweetest berries you can imagine. Each one picked at the peak of perfection. Everyday you can expect a fresh picking but you better get there early as they sell fast! We also sell the plants for your garden too.

Vegetables

Nearly 30 different kinds, from Carrots to Zucchini. Once the harvest starts you better be ready to feast on these nutritious offerings.

Tomatoes

We offer a large selection of various types, sizes and flavors. Heirloom varieties will stimulate your taste buds while the hybrids fill the needs in your kitchen. Many variations of cherry tomatoes that will bring a smile to your face.

Raspberries

We offer the classic red but we also offer Purple and Yellow types that take things to the next level.

Herbs

Basil, Parsley, Rosemary, Lemon Thyme, Dill, Chives and more. We also have Rhubarb & Horseradish.

Pumpkins

In the fall we will have Giant Atlantic Pumpkins that will make your kids laugh and your neighbors envious. Various colors and sizes will help complete your wish list. Plants also available in the spring.

Cut Flowers

Tulips, Daffodils and Alliums in the spring to Lilacs, Zinnias and Sunflowers. Depending on the season, we have many ways to satisfy your needs. Contact us for large or special orders to make that event spectacular.

Firewood

We have premium grade, seasoned Cherry that is like no other. From smoking to a hot fire, it's quality sets it apart from everything else. We have bundles for $10, 1/2 face cord for $65 to a face cord for $125. Contact us about delivery if needed.

Perennials, Shrubs and Vines

From our gardens to yours, we have many varieties available at a reasonable cost.

Free Range Eggs

Brown eggs that have been tested and washed. We keep them in our self-serve refrigerator at the Farm Stand. If you see the sign out, we have them in stock.

Jams and Jellies

We are constantly making new batches utilizing the harvests from the garden. These products are in the freezer and will hold for an entire year, if you can wait that long.

Honey

We will be replenishing our stock with this year's harvest soon. Check the updates on our website or stop by the Farm Stand to see what's available.

Baked goods

It depends on the time of year, what the garden is offering and the mood my wife is in that determines what you'll find.

Grapes

Concord and Niagra seeded grapes are the standard but we now have Seedless types from Somerset to Mars these varieties take it to the next level.

Birdhouses

Many different styles of handcrafted homes.
