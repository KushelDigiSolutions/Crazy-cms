MJB Development - Commercial Metal Buildings

If you are looking for commercial construction projects, it is important to find a company that is not only the best in the business, but also one that works with your individual needs, as well as your budget. John Carr heads up MJB Development, and has more that forty-five years in commercial construction. MJB Development Nationally Recognized

Amherst, NY, April 26, 2013 — MJB Development is pleased to announce that it received multiple honors at the recently held Star Building Systems’ National Sales Meeting in Orlando, Florida.

Star Building Systems presented MJB Development with its “2012 Master Builder Award” for the new CarMasters Collision & Glass facility in Elma, NY.  

Additionally, MJB Development and owner, John Carr, received Star’s distinguished “Outstanding Builder Award.”  This award recognizes contractors for market share and sales volume achievements during the year.  It’s the 24th year in a row that Mr. Carr has received this recognition.
